FCHS Computer Science Academy

What We Do

Fern Creek Computer Science is a technology magnet where students study in fields concerning coding and the creation of personal projects to solve problems. Here at Fern Creek, students will be learning about coding languages such as Python, JavaScript, and Swift -- these are three very important and consistently used languages that will help them in their future careers. While coding is difficult, our staff are supportive and are always willing to answers questions and help your student along the way.
